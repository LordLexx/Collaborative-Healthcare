About us

Who We are

About Us

Started by people with extensive healthcare experience and exceptional people-management skills, Collaborative Health Care (CHC) have been providing high quality healthcare services since 2014.


We are mindful that healthcare is about people - those who are being served, as well as those who serve. We believe that our goal to be one of the leaders in health care services can only be reached by keeping the promises we make and ensuring that we provide highly skilled support staff that can meet the needs of the service user. Our support staff are trained to provide care that is centred on the service user’s needs and expectations. We support people from diverse backgrounds and with different needs, lifestyles and personalities.


Collaboration, compassion, empathy, respect, integrity, dependability, accountability, transparency and excellence are core values of our business. Our culture of inclusion and caring extends to the service user’s family, friends and all other relevant professionals.

Our Pride

Qualified and Experienced Staff

We have a large bank of well qualified and experienced nurses and healthcare assistants on our books. Our staff are thoroughly vetted to ensure they meet all your needs. Providing client-focused professional services and a fast response time, we can ensure staff cover even at short notice.


Understanding that CHC’s success requires shared dedication and active participation by our staff, we support and facilitate training to continually improve our services. All services comply with the industry’s standards and legislative requirements as well as the client’s policies, procedures, and standards.



We Promise

Our Promise to Clients

Support from our clients is essential to achieving our business goals of providing service of the highest quality and being leaders in healthcare staffing. We promise to listen, consult, and act on all feedback and learnings.


We pride ourselves on the ability to structure our healthcare staffing solutions to suit each client’s needs and schedules. We promise excellence, convenience, and flexibility in all our services.
